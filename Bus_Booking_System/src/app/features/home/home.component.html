FAQs

How to book bus tickets online?
Book tickets online is 03 easy steps: Visit the web site or App Select your seat and payment option, and confirm your booking to receive your printable e-tickets
Is there any promotion for online Booking?
Yes! There are many promotions and discounts available through online booking discount codes. Look for promo codes or voucher codes that can give you a percentage discount off your booking
Can I reschedule my bus ticket?
Yes! you can reschedule your bus ticket. Bus ticket rescheduling policies vary depending on their operator, but generally, you can reschedule your ticket up to a certain time before departure(eg: 24hours) and a rescheduling fee may apply, some tickets may not have rescheduling options or have restrictions for more clarifications, check with the bus operator for their specific rescheduling policy
Can bus ticket be cancelled?
Yes! Bus ticket can be cancelled. Cancellation policies vary depending on the bus operator, but generally tickets can be cancelled up to a certain time before departure (eg: 24hours). Refunds or voucher may be issued. and a cancellation fee. Check with the bus operator for their specific cancellation policy
Which bus operators are available for online booking?
In Cameroon, some bus operators that offer online booking include, GTVC (Groupe de Transport Victoria Cameroon), STC (Société de Transport Camerounaise), Maetur, Fobus Transport, Transcam and Union des Transporteurs du Cameroun (UTC).
Which routes are available for online booking?
In Cameroon, our team provides online booking for major roads connecting urban and rural areas like: - Douala-Yaoundé - Yaoundé-Bamenda - Bamenda-Bafut - Douala-Bafoussam - Yaoundé-Garoua - Douala-Limbe - Ambam - Yaounde These roads are served by our bus operators.
